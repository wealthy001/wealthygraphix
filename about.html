Our Story

From Passion to Excellence

Wealthy Graphix Studio began as a dream to create visuals that not only look stunning but also drive real business growth.

Our Journey

What started as a small freelance operation has grown into a full-service creative studio dedicated to delivering premium digital experiences.

Wealthy Designs

It all began as Wealthy Designs, focused purely on graphic design — crafting logos, brand identities, and print materials that helped businesses stand out.

Wealthy Graphix

As demand grew, we expanded into motion and storytelling, evolving into Wealthy Graphix. This marked our entry into captivating 2D animations and dynamic visuals.

Wealthy Graphix Studio

Today, we are Wealthy Graphix Studio — a complete creative powerhouse offering animation, design, web development, and software solutions under one roof.

Meet the Founder

Akinjise Odunayo J. founded Wealthy Graphix Studio with a clear vision: to bridge creativity and technology in a way that creates lasting value for clients.

Starting with graphic design skills honed through years of passion and self-taught expertise, Odunayo expanded the studio's capabilities into animation and digital development. With a relentless focus on quality, innovation, and client success, he leads a team committed to turning ideas into wealth-building visual assets.
